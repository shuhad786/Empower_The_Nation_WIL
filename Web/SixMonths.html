Six-Month Courses

First Aid

R1500:To provide first aid awareness and basic life support.

  • Wounds and bleeding
  • Handling fractures and burns
  • Emergency scene Management
  • Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation(CPR)
  • Respiratory distress e.g. Choking, blocked airway.

Sewing

R1500: To provide alterations and new garment tailoring services.

  • Typpes of stitching
  • Threading a sewing machine
  • Sewing buttons, hems,zips, and seems
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments

Landscaping

R1500: To provide landscaping services for new and established gardens.

  • Indeginous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed structures(fountains,statues,benches,tables)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden layout

Life Skills

To provide skills to navigate basic life necessities.

  • Opening a bank account
  • Basic labour law(know your rights)
  • Basic reading and writing literacy
  • Basic numeric literacy
