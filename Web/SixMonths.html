First Aid

Learn essential first aid awareness and basic life support.

  • Wounds and bleeding
  • Burns and fractures
  • Emergency scene management
  • Cardio - Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
  • Respiratory distress e.g. choking or blocked airway

Course Fee: R1500

Sewing

Learn to provide garments and new garment tailoring services.

  • Types of stitches
  • Threading a sewing machine
  • Sewing buttons, hems, zips, and seams
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments

Course Fee: R1500

Landscaping

Learn landscaping servicing for new and established garden.

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed Structures(fountains,statues,benches,tables,built in braai's)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden layout

Course Fee: R1500

Life Skills

Learn essential life skills to navigate basic life necessities.

  • Opening a bank account
  • Basic labour law(know your rights)
  • Basic reading and writing literacy
  • Basic numeric literacy

Course Fee: R1500
